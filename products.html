The world's only active firefighting product line

Four form factors, one core technology: microencapsulated FK-5-1-12 that operates on its own at >130°C, without separate installation, power or wiring — maintaining the same firefighting power as raw capsules.

Class A, B & C(adaption) fire

PAD

Pad-type autonomous firefighting system containing microencapsulated fire extinguishing agent that detects and extinguishes fire at an early stage — a groundbreaking fire prevention product with the simplest installation method of the whole range.

  • ✓ Protects the blind spots standard fire detection misses — power-free, no additional equipment
  • ✓ Peel-and-place installation directly onto or above the risk point
  • ✓ Extinguishes Class A, B and adapted Class C fire

Typical placements: incoming & distributing boards / switches, UPS & ESS cabinets, electrical outlets and multi-plugs.

Ask About PAD
Adjustable length

WIRE

A wire-type autonomous system where microcapsules are distributed along a line shape, so the full length sprays simultaneously when triggered — maximising extinguishing performance against irregular and amorphous fire hazards.

  • ✓ Line-shape autonomous system, cut and routed to fit the object being protected
  • ✓ Built for irregular geometries standard sheet or panel formats can't follow
  • ✓ Simultaneous spray along the full protected run for high extinguishing power

Typical placements: incoming & distributing boards, electricity storage facility racking, construction facilities and underground culverts.

Ask About WIRE
Protects >300L spaces

SHIELD

SHIELD combines the advantages of PAD and WIRE into a single panel-type product — actively detecting early fires and responding preemptively to suppress the spread of damage from large fires and social disasters at the source.

  • ✓ All capsules burst simultaneously to extinguish the initial fire
  • ✓ Sized for the protection of large spaces exceeding 300 litres
  • ✓ Combines sheet and wire form factors for full-enclosure coverage

Typical placements: incoming & distributing boards / switches, communications server rooms and UPS, electricity storage facilities.

Ask About SHIELD
For the tightest spaces

FILM

FILM is applied very close to very small spaces, quickly detecting and extinguishing an initial fire. It's flexible and easy to control in thickness, so it can be fitted where sheet or panel formats simply won't.

  • ✓ Film-type autonomous system containing microencapsulated fire extinguishing agent
  • ✓ Supplied in shapes suited to the customer's specific application
  • ✓ Thin and flexible enough for direct application inside compact assemblies

Typical placements: battery pack chargers, electric wire bundles, and small enclosed spaces inside electronic assemblies.

Ask About FILM
Shared Core

Every format runs on the same known agent: FK-5-1-12

Perfluoro(2-methyl-3-pentanone) — widely used wherever water-based suppression would be impractical or could damage expensive equipment. Ozone depleting potential of zero, atmospheric lifetime of just 0.014 years, and a global warming potential under 1.
